Mastermind Game

How to play

1. Choose the symbol type: Numbers, Animals or Shapes
2. Choose the difficulty level slider
3. Pick the symbols from the palette pool or click auto pick
4. Click START GAME
5. Select and submit your guess
Results:
✔ : correct symbol in the correct position (earns 2 pts)
✔ : correct symbol in the correct position (earns 0 pt)
